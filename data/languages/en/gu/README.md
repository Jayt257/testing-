## Gujarati Script Reference

### Vowels (13 total) — for use in content
```
Independent: અ આ ઇ ઈ ઉ ઊ ઋ એ ઐ ઓ ઔ અં અઃ
Romanization: a  aa  i  ee u  oo ri  e  ai  o  au am  ah

With consonant ક (k):
ક  કા  કિ  કી  કુ  કૂ  કૃ  કે  કૈ  કો  કૌ  કં  કઃ
ka  kaa ki  kee ku  koo kri ke  kai ko  kau kam kah
```

### Consonant Groups
```
Velar  (throat): ક  ખ  ગ  ઘ  ÙŠ   (k  kh  g  gh  n-velar)
Palatal:         ચ  છ  જ  ઝ  ÙŠ   (ch chh j  zh  n-pal)
Retroflex:       ટ  ઠ  ડ  ઢ  ÙŠ   (T  Th  D  Dh  N-retro)
Dental:          ત  થ  દ  ધ  ÙŠ   (t  th  d  dh  n)
Labial:          પ  ફ  બ  ભ  ÙŠ   (p  ph  b  bh  m)
Semivowels:      ÙŠ  Ø±  ÙŠ  ÙŠ      (y  r   l  v/w)
Sibilants:       Ø´  Ø·  ÙŠ          (sh Sh  s)
Other:           Ø­  ÙŠ              (h  LL)
```

### Key Grammar Points (reference these in lesson blocks)

| Point | English | Gujarati | Notes |
|-------|---------|----------|-------|
| Sentence Order | SVO | **SOV** | Verb goes LAST |
| "is" (1st person) | I am | હ-ં ___ છ-ં | hun ___ chhun |
| "is" (3rd person) | He/She/It is | ત- ___ છ- | te ___ chhe |
| "You are" (formal) | You are | ત-મ- ___ છ- | tame ___ chho |
| Plural "are" | We are | અ-મ- ___ છ-એ | ame ___ chheye |
| Honorific "you" | You (respect) | આ-પ | aap (very formal) |
| Past tense male | Did | ___ ÙŠ-Ø© | ___-yo |
| Past tense female | Did | ___ ÙŠ- | ___-i |
| Future tense | Will | ___ ÙŠ-શ | ___-ish |
| Negation | Not | ÙŠ-Ù† / ÙŠ-Ø© | na / nahi |
