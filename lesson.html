Myocardium

/ˌmaɪəˈkɑːrdiəm/

0:04

The muscular tissue of the heart, forming the thick middle layer between the outer epicardium and the inner endocardium.

clinical_notes

Clinical Usage

"The patient presents with signs of myocardial infarction, suggesting compromised blood flow to the myocardium."

  • check_circle Used in surgical reports and pathology.
  • check_circle Refers specifically to cardiac muscle function.

lightbulb Study Tip

Root "Myo-" means muscle. Root "-cardium" means heart. Combined: Heart Muscle.
